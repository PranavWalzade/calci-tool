How to Use the Online GST Calculator

The Goods and Services Tax (GST) is an indirect tax used in India on the supply of goods and services. Our GST Calculator India is designed to simplify tax calculations for business owners, accountants, and consumers.

There are two ways to calculate GST:

  • GST Exclusive: When you have the cost of a product and you want to add the tax on top of it. Formula: GST Amount = (Original Cost * GST%) / 100.
  • GST Inclusive: When the price already includes the tax and you want to find the original value and the tax component. Formula: GST Amount = Total Price - (Total Price * (100 / (100 + GST%))).

Our tool supports all standard Indian GST slabs, including 5%, 12%, 18%, and 28%. Accurate tax calculation is essential for filing GST returns and maintaining proper business records. Use this tool for quick and error-free results.
