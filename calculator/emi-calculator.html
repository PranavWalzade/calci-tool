Understanding EMI and How It's Calculated

Equated Monthly Installment (EMI) is the fixed amount you pay back to a lender every month until your loan is fully paid off. It consists of two parts: the principal amount and the interest charged on the loan.

Our EMI Calculator India helps you plan your finances by giving you a clear picture of your monthly commitments. Whether you are looking for a home loan, car loan, or a personal loan, knowing your EMI in advance is crucial for budgeting. The formula used for calculation is [P x R x (1+R)^N]/[(1+R)^N-1], where P is the principal, R is the monthly interest rate, and N is the number of monthly installments.

Using this free tool, you can experiment with different interest rates and tenures to find a loan plan that fits your pocket. We recommend keeping your total EMIs below 40-50% of your monthly income for financial stability.
